The series "5:32" is about the story of Shalkar, who was the best investigator in the Department for Especially Serious Crimes, and now he is an ordinary pensioner. Chases, shootings, detentions of serial killers have remained in the distant past, the main character tries to brighten up quiet and peaceful everyday life with alcohol and memories. He misses the eventful days. It was not easy for the investigator to face brutal murders every day, to go to the corpses, to look into the eyes of the relatives of the unfortunate victims. Despite all the difficulties, Shalkar loved work, which became the meaning of his life for him. The detective gradually sank down, taking more and more often to the bottle. Everything changed with the appearance of a young investigator on his doorstep. A policeman came to ask Shalkar for help. There has been a series of murders in the city, it is not yet possible to calculate the maniac. Employees of the department found out that the criminal's handwriting is surprisingly similar to the serial killer who operated in the city in the mid-90s. This case was led by Shalkar, but he never managed to catch the villain. Perhaps in tandem with a young partner, he will get to the truth, and the serial killer will be punished as he deserves.
