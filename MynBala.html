About the film "Myn Bala: Warriors of the stepp":

The film "Myn Bala: Warriors of the stepp" (Myn Bala) tells about the bloody events of the beginning of the XVIII century, when the very existence of the Kazakh people was under threat destruction. At such a time, only the common will of the entire people, who have rejected internal strife and disagreements, can bring deliverance from foreign invasion. But even with the full gathering of all their warriors, the Kazakhs may still be defeated in this war. This is the great moment when the defense of the Fatherland all those who are able to carry weapons stand up. Young beardless youths also gather their army and, under the leadership of the brave brave Sartai, without fear and they engage in an unequal battle with the enemy. This army (Myn Bala translates from Kazakh as a thousand children) turns out to be so formidable that it affects the outcome of a merciless war. In their young hearts there is a true love for the motherland, inspiring great deeds. Realistic battle scenes in the film are impressive, and the landscapes of the Kazakh steppes and mountains fascinate with their beauty. The melody from the famous song of Abai very clearly conveys the nature of the moments and does not leave indifferent throughout the film.
